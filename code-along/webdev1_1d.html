Authentic Sinigang na Baboy Recipe

Sinigang na Baboy

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) pork ribs or pork belly, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 medium onion, peeled and quartered
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 radish (labanos), peeled and sliced
  • 1 eggplant (talong), sliced into rounds
  • 6-8 pieces okra, ends trimmed
  • 2-3 pieces green chili peppers (siling haba)
  • 1 bundle kangkong (water spinach) or spinach, stems removed
  • 1 packet (40g) sinigang sa sampalok mix (tamarind soup base mix)
  • 8-10 cups water
  • Fish sauce (patis) or salt, to taste
  • Ground black pepper, to taste
